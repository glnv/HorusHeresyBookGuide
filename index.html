This is not the full fifty-four volume chronicle of the Heresy — it is the sanctioned compliance path, drawn from review scores and community consensus rather than one reader's judgement alone. It carries you through the best of the saga: the plot-critical spine, each primarch's own account entered into the record at the exact hour his legion turns, and the side campaigns worth the detour along the way. Three off-world postings — Eisenhorn, Night Lords, and Gaunt's Ghosts — are scheduled into the quieter administrative stretches so the vigil doesn't stall. Entries marked optional still hold their place in the path — skip one and nothing later goes unexplained. Volumes struck from the record entirely are a different matter: they sit sealed in the archive below, and reinstating one means finding its place yourself. Mark an entry complete and the servitors note it for the record.
